Meet the Characters

Cinderella

A kind and beautiful young girl who believes in dreams and magic, despite all her hardships.

Fairy Godmother

A magical figure who helps Cinderella attend the royal ball with a little bit of magic and kindness.

Stepmother

The cruel woman who treats Cinderella unfairly and tries to stop her from achieving happiness.

Prince Charming

The handsome prince who falls in love with Cinderella and changes her life forever.
